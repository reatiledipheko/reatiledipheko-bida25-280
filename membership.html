FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I cancel my membership at any time?

Yes — all memberships are month-to-month. You can cancel any time with no penalty. Simply give us one month's notice before your next billing date.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time by speaking to our front desk team. Changes take effect from your next billing cycle.

Is there a joining fee?

There is a once-off joining fee of P50 for all new members. This covers your access card and an introductory fitness assessment.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

Yes — we offer 15% off for full-time students with a valid student card, and corporate packages for companies with 5 or more employees. Contact us for details.
